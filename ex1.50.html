Rajalakshmi institue Of Technology

ABOUT:


Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology is an engineering college in Tamil Nadu, India.
It is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai, and accredited by AICTE, NBA.
This college opened in 2008 and it was originally in Thiruvallur.
